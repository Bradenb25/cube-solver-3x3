Privacy Policy

Cube Solver 3x3 · Last updated June 26, 2026

Your privacy matters. The short version: Cube Solver 3x3 does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything the app does happens on your device.

Information we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no backend server. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, or any analytics or usage data.

Camera

The app uses your camera only to read the colors of your Rubik's Cube when you choose to scan it. Images are processed entirely on your device to detect the cube's colors. Photos are not uploaded, stored, or shared with us or anyone else.

Data stored on your device

App settings, your premium unlock status, the count of free scans used, and your timer history are saved locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Purchases

The optional one-time Premium purchase is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Apple's Privacy Policy governs that transaction.

Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs. We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
