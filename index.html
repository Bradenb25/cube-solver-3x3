Frequently Asked Questions

How do I solve my cube?

Tap Solve a cube, then either paint your scramble on the 2D net or tap the camera icon to scan each face. Once the cube is entered, tap Solve to watch an animated 3D solution play out move by move.

How does the camera scan work?

Hold each face in the on-screen box and tap Capture. The app reads the colors on your device and assembles the cube. You get a few free scans to try it; unlimited scanning is part of Premium.

What's included in Premium?

A one-time purchase unlocks unlimited camera scans, the full CFOP reference (F2L, OLL & PLL), and the complete pattern library. Buy once — yours forever.

I bought Premium but it's locked / I reinstalled.

Open the Premium screen and tap Restore purchase. As long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID, your unlock will come back.

The scan detected a wrong color.

On the confirmation screen, tap any sticker to cycle its color before tapping Use face. You can also fine-tune everything on the 2D net before solving.
